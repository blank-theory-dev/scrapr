The Next-Gen Duty Glove Supercharged by Graphex.

The ENFORCR+ Ghost® is an award-winning duty glove engineered for Law Enforcement personnel. Born from the real-world needs of Law-Enforcement professionals, that demanded a glove with 360-degree cut protection and unmatched dexterity for handling weapons and entry tools. Meet ENFORCR+ Ghost: The Next-Gen Duty Glove you've been waiting for, supercharged by Graphex® for superior strength and flexibility. This solution encapsulates innovation with practicality, offering a glove that not only meets the demanding needs of tactical and duty operations but sets a new benchmark in protective gear.

Achieving EN 388 certification and the highest cut rating (Level F) through Graphex® yarn technology, it offers 360-degree cut protection without compromising dexterity. The strategic reinforcement between the thumb and forefinger enhances durability for high-wear tasks. With a micro sandy nitrile coating, these gloves provide exceptional grip in any condition—wet, dry, or oily. Touch-screen compatibility keeps you connected without sacrificing safety, while the contact heat level 1 protection withstands temperatures up to 100°C for 15 seconds. The discreet design in subdued colours and short wrist construction ensure seamless integration with duty gear.

Features

  • Graphex® cut-resistant yarn for maximum protection
  • EN 388 certified for abrasion resistance over 8000 cycles
  • Micro sandy nitrile coating for exceptional grip
  • Short wrist design for enhanced mobility
  • Strategic reinforcement between thumb and forefinger
  • Touch-screen compatible
  • Contact Heat Level 1 protection up to 100°C for 15 seconds
  • Discreet subdued colours for tactical operations
  • The ENFORCR+ Ghost® is an Australian Good Design Award Winner
